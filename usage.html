Usage Guidance

You can find out how to use this self-learning module in this page.
'Self-learning Module - Logic':
Clicking on it will redirect you to the starting page, which houses all the problems within the logic topic.
From there, you can navigate to any specific problem by clicking the corresponding right-hand icon.
'Home'
It contains the description of the whole specific problem.

Hints
When you encounter a situation where you cannot proceed or become stuck, accessing hints with your suitable conditions allows you to move forward.
It's highly advisable to begin by reading the hint and then attempt to move on independently.
Reviewing the answer for the hint should be considered the last step.

'Full Solution'
You can find the full solution for the specificc problem by clicking it.

What's the goal/meaning of this self-learning module?
By utilizing this module, you'll gain a deeper grasp of providing Natural Deduction through independent thinking, with hints provided to guide further exploration, and ultimately relish the satisfaction of successfully resolving the issue.
If you cannot get the final right answer, please don't look at the full solution directly. Otherwise, you'll gain the least in this module.

Have your final answer?
You can check it by clicking the 'Full Solution' button.

Get stucked?
You can jump to hints page by clicking 'Hints'. Then, go for a specific hint that suitable for your condition.
